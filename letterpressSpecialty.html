Letterpress Specialty.

We've been doing letterpress ever since our opening in 1924, so I can assure you that we have a good bit more specialty than most in this area. Whether you're a designer desiring that "retro" look for a client or a bride-to-be wishing for a unique save-the-date card, we can help. No templates here, it's uniquely you!

How do we use letterpress?

We handle a wide range of printing projects — from coasters and invitations to calling cards and informal notes — and proudly deliver work like this every day.

Additionally, our extensive letterpress department allows us to do "extras" many other printing companies fail to deliver. These include printing jobs like Numbering, Channel Scoring, and even Die-Cutting!

Lastly, for the technical-minded, we have an 8" x 12" C&P Platten Press, two 13" x 19" Kluges, two Miehle Verticals, and a 20" x 26″ Miller Simplex Cylinder Press in our letterpress arsenal. We also offer limited hot metal typecasting capabilities using our Ludlow typecaster for select fonts. Moreover, we, of course, welcome modern designs; we can produce custom letterpress dies directly from your Illustrator, InDesign, and PDF Files!
